Travel To Ayodhya

The Birth Place Of Shree Ram.

This place is the birth place of "Shree Ram". In Hindu scared scriptures it is stated that upon the arrival of Baghwaan Ram after defeating Ravan people celebrate by lighting "Deep".

People who wants to calm their minds and feel the inner peace must visit this place.

Recently a new temple of Shree Ram is built after a long debate in Indian Supreme Court regarding the proof that Shree Ram was born in Ayodhya.

Almost after 500 years this decision was taken. It is belived that when mughal ruled in India they destroyed the Indian temples and built their mosques. Afterwards the hindus fought against this and managed to present proof that Shree Ram was indeed born in Ayodhya.
